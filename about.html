About Us

BACKGROUND

Guide is a grassroot level NGO promoted in the year 1995 with the main objective of minimizing the sufferings of the poor and disadvantaged people with specific focus on women and children. Guide is registered under Indian Societies Registration Act XX! Of 1860. Guide is also registered under Income Tax Exemption Act 1961, under sections 12A and 80G and with NITI AYOG (Darpan Registration no… ) and CSR registration act …..


VISION

A sustainable, gender sensitive, child friendly and eco-conscious society with adequate food, shelter, good health and secured life for all.


MISSION

To reduce the vulnerability of people especially women and children to social, economic, cultural and health risks and provide a healthy, peaceful and secured life.


GEOGRAPHIC COVERAGE

Krishna and NTR districts of Andhra Pradesh. Guide is now working with about 10,000 families in these districts through various programs.


TARGET COMMUNITIES

Women, Children, Small and marginal farmers with specific focus on SC, ST, OBC and economically backward communities.


TARGET POPULATION

Women, Children, Small and marginal farmers


THEMATIC AREAS

Women and Child development

Community Health

WASH

Sustainable Agriculture

Livelihoods

Climate Change adaptation and Mitigation
